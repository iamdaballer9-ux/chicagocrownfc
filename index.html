About the Club

Chicago Crown FC develops confident, technical players while emphasizing teamwork and sportsmanship. Our coaches focus on long-term player development and a pathway to competitive youth leagues.

Mission

To create a soccer-first environment that builds character, skills, and community — on and off the field.

Core Values

  • Respect & Sportsmanship
  • Hard Work & Development
  • Community Engagement
